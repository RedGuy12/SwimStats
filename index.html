What is my swimmer ID?

Please note that I only have access to one Team Unify site, and I have no idea how much it differs from other Team Unify sites. It might differ a lot, or it might not differ at all. I don't know. So, unforunately, these instructions might not work, depending on how your Team Unify site works.

  1. Go to your team's page on Team Unify.
  2. Sign in.
  3. Go to your "My account" page.
  4. Click on the "Members" tab.
  5. Right-click on the swimmer you want to generate statistics for and choose "View image", "Open image in new tab", or the equivalent for your browser.
  6. Find the swimmer ID (the series of random numbers typically at the end of the URL). Some examples:
    • https://www.teamunify.com/rest/ondeck/member/photo/cdn/reczzatst/4305991 -> 4305991
  7. Click "Generate stats!"
